Chief Engineers

Cory Koseck (7ark) | Chief Architect
 Cory is head of the Architecture Division here at Sunburn BayTM and put care into all facets of the park's design. From a broad overview of how the park should feel, to the design of the very paths you'll walk on, Cory worked to make sure you feel at home here at Sunburn Bay.TM

Coachi | Chief Design Coordinator
 Coachi is the Chief of Design, and put care into every nook and cranny you'll see here at Sunburn Bay.TM They handled not only the literal construction of the park, but also the overall design and vision for all of the beautiful pieces of the world you'll enter when you visit Sunburn Bay.TM

Deadknife | Chief of Construction
 Deadknife leads as our Chief of Construction, and has put their talented efforts towards creating many of the wonderful pieces you'll see all throughout Sunburn Bay.TM They handle the construction of all the rides you'll see, as well as many of the smaller details throughout, such as trash cans, lamp posts, reclining chairs, and even the beloved statue of Bongo the Kangaroo.

Outside Contractors

Yanzl | Construction Contractor
 Yanzl provided many of our set pieces for the world of Sunburn Bay,TM including many of our rock fixtures, exotic plant life, and more.

Jeremy Cook | Logo Designer
 Jeremy was contracted to create unique signs to bring life to the park, and he excelled. Creating not only the Sunburn BayTM logo, he created many of the parks shops and ride signs, bringing fun and joy to the park's atmosphere.

Shorebound | Audio Engineer
 Shorebound worked with the architecture team to create the perfect sounds to aid in your vacation experience. Listen to the soft melodies in our exoctic cave areas, or the fun upbeat tunes while you grab a bite to eat, Shorebound helped to craft these experiences.

Stein | Directory Artwork Creator
 Stein is another excellent artist who worked to created the logo for the 'Dolphin Directory' and helped to concept out a few of our shop designs.
